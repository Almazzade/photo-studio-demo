SERVICES

Wedding

Wedding photography includes more than 200 photos and videos of the bride and groom. The video consists of 15-minute short videos.

New born

Newborn shoot includes baby clothes, makeup, over 100 photos and videos. There are special clothes for girls.

Birthday

Birthday shoot includes room decoration, dj, balloons. Underwater shooting is also included in the seascape shooting.

Henna

In our henna shoots, you can choose a female or male photographer. It takes more than 150 pictures before the henna painting is finished.

Party

During the party shooting, there is unlimited photo shooting from the time the decorations are set up until the party is over.

Engagement

Our engagement shoots include more than 200 photos from the ring ceremony of the bride and groom to the end.

PORTFOLIO

WEDDING

Wedding photography is not for everyone. And yet there are a variety of styles allowing each photographer to find their own way of fitting into this specialty.

NEW BORN

Newborn babies are best photographed within the first 10 days, whilst they are very sleepy and happy to pose for us. These sessions can become very difficult after the baby is 14 days old.

BIRTHDAY

When it comes to your birthday photoshoot, preparation is key. These will be photos that you can hang in your home, share on your social media, and even hand down to your kids.

PARTY

A photoshoot party is always a good idea! When you want to feel like a superstar ready to hit the red carpet. Since friends make everything better, gather your squad.

ABOUT US

Founded in 2007 by renowned advertising photographer North Sullivan, TPS blurs the line between editorial wedding photography and personal portraiture. Located in three retro warehouses in Sydney.

Our mission is to help people to see beauty in themselves while creating wonderful works of art that will be passed down through generations.
